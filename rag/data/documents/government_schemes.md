# Government Agricultural Schemes in India

## PM-KISAN Scheme
- **Amount**: ₹6,000 per year (₹2,000 every 4 months)
- **Eligibility**: All landholding farmer families
- **How to Apply**: Visit nearest CSC or apply online at pmkisan.gov.in
- **Documents Required**: Aadhaar, land records, bank account details

## Crop Insurance (PMFBY)
- **Coverage**: Crop loss due to natural calamities, pest attacks
- **Premium**: 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi
- **Claim**: Up to ₹20,000 per hectare
- **How to Apply**: Through bank or CSC

## Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
- **Loan Amount**: Up to ₹3 lakh at concessional interest rate
- **Interest Rate**: 4% per annum (effective after subsidy)
- **Documents**: Land records, Aadhaar, passport photo

## Fertilizer Subsidy
- **Urea**: Heavily subsidized (~₹500/bag)
- **DAP**: Subsidized (~₹1,350/bag)
- **NPK**: Available at subsidized rates
- **Where to Buy**: Authorized fertilizer dealers

## Agricultural Equipment (SMAM)
- **Subsidy**: 40-60% on farm machinery
- **Equipment**: Tractors, harvesters, seed drills, sprayers
- **How to Apply**: Through state agriculture department

## Solar Pump Scheme
- **Subsidy**: Up to 90% for small farmers
- **Capacity**: 2HP to 10HP
- **Application**: State energy department or bank

## PM-KUSUM Scheme
- Solar pumps for farmers
- Grid-connected solar power plants
- Subsidy for setting up renewable energy

## Interest Subvention Scheme
- Crop loans at concessional rates
- 2% interest concession for timely repayment
- Additional 3% incentive for prompt payment
