# VaagAI - Smart Farming Platform

## About VaagAI
VaagAI is an intelligent smart farming platform designed to help Indian farmers make data-driven decisions. Our mission is to empower farmers with technology, weather insights, market information, and expert guidance.

## Mission
To transform Indian agriculture through AI-powered solutions, making farming more profitable, sustainable, and accessible for every farmer.

## Our Services

### 1. Smart Crop Planning
- AI-based crop recommendations based on soil, climate, and market data
- Seasonal crop calendars
- Rotation planning

### 2. Weather Forecasting
- 7-day weather forecast
- Rain alerts and monsoon tracking
- Frost and heat wave warnings
- SMS and app notifications

### 3. Market Prices
- Real-time mandi prices from across India
- Price trends and analytics
- Sell recommendations
- Direct buyer connections

### 4. Disease Diagnosis
- AI-powered crop disease detection
- Treatment recommendations
- Pesticide and fertilizer suggestions

### 5. Irrigation Management
- Smart irrigation scheduling
- Water usage optimization
- Drip and sprinkler guidance

### 6. Government Schemes
- Latest agricultural subsidies
- Application guidance
- Document preparation
