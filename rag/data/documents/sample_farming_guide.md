# VaagAI Farm Advisory Document

## Government Schemes for Farmers

### PM-KISAN Scheme
The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) provides Rs. 6000 per year to farmer families.
- Eligible: All landholding farmer families
- Payment: Rs. 2000 every 4 months
- Apply: Through local patwari or online at pmkisan.gov.in

### Kisan Credit Card
Provides credit at concessional rates for agricultural activities.
- Credit limit: Based on landholding
- Interest rate: 4% for timely repayment
- Maximum limit: Rs. 3 lakh

### Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana
Crop insurance scheme protecting against crop loss.
- Premium: 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi
- Coverage: Both yield loss and prevented sowing

## Crop Management

### Rice Cultivation
1. **Land Preparation**: Flood fields and create puddles
2. **Variety Selection**: Choose based on region (Basmati, Paddy, etc.)
3. **Sowing**: Transplant seedlings at 20-25 days
4. **Water Management**: Maintain 5cm water depth
5. **Harvesting**: When 80% grains turn golden

### Wheat Cultivation
1. **Soil**: Loamy soil preferred, pH 6.5-7.5
2. **Sowing Time**: October-November (Rabi season)
3. **Irrigation**: 4-5 critical stages
4. **Harvesting**: When grains harden and moisture is 14%

## Pest Management

### Common Pests and Control
- **Stem Borer**: Use trap crops, apply Chlorantraniliprole
- **Brown Planthopper**: Use resistant varieties, avoid excess nitrogen
- **Aphids**: Apply Neem oil or Imidacloprid

### Organic Pest Control
- Neem oil spray (5ml per liter water)
- Garlic-pepper decoction
- Trapping with yellow sticky cards

## Fertilizer Application

### NPK Guidelines
- **Nitrogen**: Split application (50% basal, 50% top dressing)
- **Phosphorus**: Apply as basal dose
- **Potassium**: Apply based on soil test

### Organic Fertilizers
- Farmyard Manure: 10-15 tonnes/hectare
- Vermicompost: 5 tonnes/hectare
- Neem cake: 250 kg/hectare

## Irrigation Methods

### Drip Irrigation
- Water efficiency: 90%
- Suitable for: Vegetables, fruits, cash crops
- Spacing: Based on crop spacing

### Sprinkler Irrigation
- Water efficiency: 70-80%
- Suitable for: Field crops, lawns
- Pressure: 2-4 kg/cm²

## Market Information

### Minimum Support Prices (MSP)
Government fixes MSP for major crops before each season.
- Paddy: Rs. 2183/quintal
- Wheat: Rs. 2275/quintal
- Mustard: Rs. 5050/quintal

### Selling Platforms
- APMC Mandis
- e-NAM portal
- Direct procurement by government agencies
